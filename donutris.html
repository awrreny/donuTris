donutris

donutris is very cool because you can do many things that you can't normally do in tetris. for example, you can:

  1. eat the donut
  2. spin the donut
  3. twist the donut
  4. do a horizontal bound left or right shift on the donut
  5. you can even spell the word doughnut correctly if you feel like it!
